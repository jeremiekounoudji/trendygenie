# TrendyGenie Product Overview

TrendyGenie is a Flutter-based marketplace application connecting customers with service providers/businesses.

## Core Functionality
- User authentication (customers and providers)
- Business/company registration and management
- Service listings and discovery
- Location-based service search
- Provider dashboard for business management
- Order and payment processing

## User Types
- **Customer**: Browse and book services
- **Provider**: Manage businesses, services, and orders
- **Admin**: Platform administration

## Key Flows
- Registration with multi-step onboarding (type selection → company details → certification)
- Provider certification workflow (pending → approved/rejected/suspended)
- Service discovery via categories and location
